# PRISMA-S Checklist (Search Reporting)

Completed per Rethlefsen et al. (2021). *PRISMA-S: an extension to the
PRISMA statement for reporting literature searches in systematic reviews.*

Fill in the **Section/Page** column with the location in your thesis.
