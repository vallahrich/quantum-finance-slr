## Scope of review

The search strategy was reviewed iteratively with the thesis supervisor
during protocol development (v1.0–v3.7). The final Boolean query
(Block 1: quantum computing terms × Block 2: finance terms) was refined
across five search iterations (v1–v5) with sensitivity checks against
a 20-paper benchmark set. The supervisor confirmed the scope boundaries,
database selection (Scopus, OpenAlex, arXiv, Semantic Scholar), and
date limits (2016–present).

## Limitations

No formal information-specialist / librarian review was conducted.
The search strategy was reviewed only by the thesis supervisor. This is
documented as a limitation in the thesis methods chapter (§3.5).
The benchmark sensitivity check (20 known-relevant papers, recall ≥ 95%)
partially compensates for the absence of specialist review.
