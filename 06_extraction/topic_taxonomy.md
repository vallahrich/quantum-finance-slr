# Topic Taxonomy

Draft controlled taxonomy for LLM-assisted thematic coding.

## Controlled Topics

- `portfolio_optimization`: Asset allocation, portfolio construction, rebalancing, or portfolio selection.
- `derivative_pricing`: Option pricing, derivative valuation, structured products, Greeks.
- `risk_management`: Market risk, credit risk, CVaR, stress testing, exposure analysis.
- `fraud_and_detection`: Fraud detection, AML, anomaly detection in financial systems.
- `forecasting_and_prediction`: Time-series forecasting, return prediction, regime prediction.
- `trading_and_execution`: Trading strategies, execution, market microstructure.
- `insurance_and_actuarial`: Insurance, actuarial science, underwriting, reinsurance.
- `credit_and_lending`: Credit scoring, default prediction, lending decisions.
- `quantum_ml_for_finance`: QML methods used for explicitly financial tasks.
- `optimization_methods`: Generic optimization methods with an explicit finance application.
- `simulation_and_monte_carlo`: Monte Carlo, amplitude estimation, stochastic simulation.
- `benchmarking_and_advantage`: Benchmarking, utility, complexity, or advantage claims in finance.
